About OROMS's Restaurant

Bellisio Foods is collaborating with OROMS® Grill & Bar to bring a line of frozen single-serve entrées and multi-serve meals under the OROMS® licensed brand to the frozen food category. Bellisio Foods’ Research & Development and OROMS® Culinary Team teamed up to develop the bold, southwestern flavor profiles for the new frozen food line. The frozen food category has been frozen in time. This lack of innovation has made consumers grow weary of the same flavor combinations, nutritional components and even packaging. Bellisio Foods, through its collaboration with OROMS, saw an excellent opportunity to deliver “bold” in a big way. From bold flavors to bold packaging, Fresh’s frozen entrees are sure to be eye-catching as well as palate-pleasing. Fresh® frozen entrees will help define the future of frozen meals and infuse a sleepy category with new energy, innovation and excitement.
